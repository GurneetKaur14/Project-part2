Acuarela Events Inc in Land O Lakes

Maria Boyer De Portillo was born in Venezuela. Her passion for floral arrangements started since she was young, running a local flower shop on the coast of Lake Maracaibo in 1966. Since then, she dedicated herself to floral decorations for any type of event, preparing romantic, exotic, and modern decoration according to the requirements of the customers. In 2007 Maria moved with her family to the Tampa Bay area and opened Acuarela Events-Florist. Professionalism and dedication have allowed her to thrive for over three decades, continuously offering exceptional services.
